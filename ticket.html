Ticket New Redesign

This is a Redesign of movie ticket booking app 'Ticket New'. I borrowed their original primary color 'Gorse' and gave it simple and minimal design with visuals to balance space in view. The design was done Sketch and made prototype in Principle

Landing View

Showcase new release in horizontal image scroll, all current movies can be selected from vertical view and Movie available on the particular date can be checked.

Movie View

Pick Movie date, seats, time and Theater

Pick Seat

User Can Choose Their Desired Seats.

Tickets

Users can view Their current Tickets as well old ones. Each ticket is given unique barcode which is scanned at entry at theater for easy verification

Created Using Principle
